
POS architecture

the pos is just a client (terminal) into the core ordering system used by staff to create and manage orders

in our design pos is just a client, similar to online order app, it communicates with orders system

                      [POS] -- -- [Ordering (headless)] -- -- [Web / Mobile Client]

                      [Ordering]
                        - Payments
                        - Orders
                        - Receipts
                        - Send emails
                        - Queuing
                        - DB access
                        - Menus
                        - Line items

                      [Web / Mobile]
                        - handles customers ordering

                      [POS]
                        - staff access
                        - staff checkins
                        - station configs
                        - menu item placements
                        - add to menus
                        - create orders
                        - void orders
                        - receive cash payments
                        - receive credit card payments
                        - print receipts (thermal)

                      [Ext. Apps]
                        - Call Center (AI)
                        - Delivery Tools
                        - Accounting
                        - Reservations
                        - Kiosk
                        - Websites
                        - Suppliers
                        - Loyalty Program
                        - Staffing
                        - Social Media
